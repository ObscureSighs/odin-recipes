Spam Musubi Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 Can of Spam
  • 6 cups of Sushi Rice
  • Nori Roasted Seaweed
  • Soy Sauce
  • 6 Eggs
  • Red Pepper Flakes

Cooking Instructions

  1. Make the dipping sauce, add soy sauce and red pepper flake in a side dish (Simple but Delicious)
  2. Make 6 Cups of Sushi Rice
  3. Open a can of Spam, and slice it into 8-10 slices
  4. Dip the Spam into some soy sauce and cook it in a pan
  5. Fry the eggs (overhard)
  6. Place a strip of the seaweed shiny side down on a cutting board
  7. Place your musubi mold onto the center of the seaweed
  8. Add rice to the mold firmly pressing it in until you have 1-1.5in worth of rice
  9. Add a fried over-hard egg to the top then lift the mold
  10. Add the spam on top and then roll it up in the seaweed
  11. Serve warm, and enjoy the Hawaiian taste of musubi
