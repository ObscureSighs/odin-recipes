Carne Asada Tacos Recipe

Ingrediants

  • 2 Avocados
  • 3 Tbsp Cojita Cheese
  • 1/3 Cup Diced Onions
  • 1/2 Cup Chopped Cilantro
  • 6 Tortillas
  • Limes (Optional)
  • Carne Asada(Grilled Steak, Your Preferance)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Grill the Carne Asada
  2. Mash and then spread the avocados across the tortilla
  3. Add the carne asada
  4. Finish topping the taco with the cojita cheese, diced onions and cilantro
  5. Finish by squezing some lime juice
