Carne Asada Tacos Recipe

Ingrediants

  • 2 Avocados
  • 3 Tbsp Cojita Cheese
  • 1/3 Cup Diced Onions
  • 1/2 Cup Chopped Cilantro
  • 6 Tortillas
  • Limes (Optional)
  • Carne Asada(Grilled Steak, Your Preferance)

Grill your Carne Asada to your preferance, then mash the avocados. Once mashed spread the avocado on the tortilla. Add the carne asada on top, and add some cojita cheese, diced onions, and cilantro on top. Then squeeze some lime jucie and enjoy!
