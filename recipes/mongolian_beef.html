My Mongolian Beef Recipe

Ingrediants

  • 1lb Thinnly Sliced Beef (Steak, Your Preferance)
  • 1 Head of Broccoli
  • Cooked Rice
  • 1/4 Cup Soy Sauce
  • Packet of Sunbird Mongolian Seasoning
  • 2 Tbsp Oil
  • 1 Bunch Green Onion
  • 2 Tbsps Water

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cobine the seasoning, soy sauce, and water
  2. Heat the oil in a wok (or large pan)
  3. Add the meat and cook until it's browned on all sides
  4. Once the meat is ready add the sauce mixture and broccoli and cook until the sauce thickens
  5. Add the green onions and serve. YUM!
